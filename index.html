Password Master is very efficient tool for generating strong and unpredicatble random passwords.


Keep your accounts safe and avoid security breaches on your social handles, websites, affiliate pages,digital lockers etc. You can also copy the generated password to your clipboard. You can also create a list of your passwords used for various accounts,sites etc.


Add the site or account you are saving password for, enter your custom password or generate a brand new,add it and save it. You can also DOWNLOAD your password list as pdf.
